# CP3/CP4/CP5/CP6 Status

Date: 2026-05-10

## Completed

CP3 through CP6 are implemented and verified.

### CP3: persisted route-proof/context ABI

- `scripts/mome_moce_harness.py` now validates route proofs and frontier packets during benchmark runs.
- `--write-artifacts <dir>` writes first-class per-case artifacts:
  - `route_proofs/<case_id>.json`;
  - `frontier_packets/<case_id>.json`;
  - `index.json` with schema hashes, per-artifact SHA-256 hashes, selected IDs, and max evidence items.
- Route proofs and packets now carry `case_id` when emitted by benchmark runs.
- Frontier packets include a `context_budget` object with max selected items, selected count, packet tokens, tokens avoided, and overflow policy.
- `validate_route_artifacts()` enforces schema validity plus semantic consistency:
  - selected IDs match proof and packet evidence;
  - packet and proof answerability agree;
  - referenced evidence IDs exist in the corpus;
  - packet evidence does not exceed budget;
  - non-answerable outputs do not carry selected evidence.

### CP4: compactness and capacity enforcement

- Evidence selection now uses a query-derived evidence budget instead of filling to top-k.
- `evaluate_case()` now enforces compactness as a pass/fail condition.
- Default max evidence is derived from gold labels:
  - no-context and abstain cases: 0;
  - answerable cases: number of required evidence IDs.
- Selector improvements:
  - decoy questions prioritize conflicted decoys plus authoritative counterparts;
  - stale/current questions preserve needed contrast pairs;
  - support/filler records are skipped as active evidence;
  - artifact-path, memory-advisory, benchmark ctx, workflow, and calculation/write queries get targeted utility priority;
  - `ctx 8192` is normalized as an anchor equivalent to `ctx=8192`.
